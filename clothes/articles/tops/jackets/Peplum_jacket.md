A **peplum jacket** is a [jacket](jacket "wikilink") with a short
[overskirt](overskirt "wikilink") known as a
*[peplum](overskirt "wikilink")* attached. Peplum jackets experienced a
revival as a fashion trend in 2012.[1][2]
